About Steel Cup Cafe

Welcome to Steel Cup Cafe, a cozy and welcoming cafe located in the heart of Long Beach. We specialize in serving artisanal coffee, delicious pastries, and homemade breakfast and lunch items. Our friendly staff is dedicated to providing excellent customer service, and we take pride in using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients in all of our dishes.

Our Menu

Artisanal Coffee

Our coffee is carefully selected and roasted to perfection. Try our signature espresso or latte, or choose from our wide selection of specialty drinks.

Pastries

Our freshly baked pastries are made from scratch every day. From croissants to muffins to danishes, we have something for everyone.

Breakfast and Lunch

Our breakfast and lunch items are made with only the freshest ingredients. Try our famous avocado toast or our hearty breakfast burrito.

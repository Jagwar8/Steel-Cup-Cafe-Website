About Steel Cup Café

Welcome to Steel Cup Café, a breakfast and lunch spot located in the heart of Artcroft Manor in Long Beach. Ever since opening in 2019, we have served artisan sandwiches, wraps, salads, brunch plates, and fresh-baked pastries to our community. Our executive chef, Marc Chitwood, brings quality and craftsmanship to our interpretations of beloved café favorites, while Nicholas Stok's coffee program offers a selection of specialty drinks and organic roasts that are Fair Trade and Rainforest Alliance certified. The café has an unexpected blend of rustic and raw, organic and industrial atmosphere, making it a perfect locale for work or play. Our approach to creating a modern yet timeless café experience speaks for itself, having earned us a 5-star rating on Yelp. Steel Cup Café is an environmentally sustainable business that partners with local and small-scale farmers, operates energy-efficient technology, and uses 100% biodegradable, plant-based alternatives instead of single-use plastics.
